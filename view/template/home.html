SALVATION COMES THROUGH A CROSS AND A CRUCIFIED CHRIST.

We are a warm group of Christians who are striving to worship God in the same way that the first century Christians did.

In this way we are striving to continue the divine unity that Jesus prayed for (John 17v 21). The church of Christ has congregations throughout the United Kingdom and all over the world. However we have no central organization on earth, but we have a common cause: to be the Church as it was when first instituted by the Lord Jesus Christ.

More over the Bible teaches us that our practices and teachings must be by the authority of our Lord Jesus Christ. Whatever you do, whether in word or in deed, do it all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks to God the Father through him (Colossians 3v17)..

Psalms 1: 1 Blessed is the man who walks not in the counsel of the ungodly, Nor stands in the path of sinners, Nor sits in the seat of the scornful..
